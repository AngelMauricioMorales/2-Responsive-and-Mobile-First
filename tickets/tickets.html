HOIST THE SAILS!

October 1st & 2nd, 2015 in Ghent Belgium

ArrrrCamp is a conference for the modern developer interested in Ruby, Rail or the industry in general. Our event will inspire you, help you learn new things, meet new people and hang out with old friends.

A FANTASTIC VENUE

ArrrrCamp will take place at the Handelsbeurs. This amazing concert hall with big lounge area is located smack in the middle of Ghent's city center within walking distance of the old city parts and with plentry of hotels, restaurants and pubs nearby. Our infamous public transportation will get you anywhere you need to be. Coming by car? We have an underground car park at our doorstep.
